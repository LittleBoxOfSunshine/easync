Technical Overview

Our team set out to create the perfect group scheduling application: one that could take the schedules of multiple members and produce ideal meeting times for everyone.

Target Audience

Easync is for the busy individual who needs an easier way to collaborate with groups. Professional or personal, it's aimed at the kind of person that may be unable to answer the question "When can we meet again?"

Project Requirements

These are some things we felt were absolutely necessary to include in the project.
  • Google Calendar integration
  • Ideal meeting algorithm
  • Group collaboration
